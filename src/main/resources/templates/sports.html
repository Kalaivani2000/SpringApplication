Silambattam.

Silambam is a well-known martial art style that is practised with specialised weapons, typically bamboo sticks.

Kabaddi

The core idea of the game is to score points by raiding into the opponent's court and touching as many defense players as possible without getting caught on a single breath.

Hockey

Hockey is an outdoor game played between two teams of 11 players who use long curved sticks to hit a small ball and try to score goals.

Cricket

Real Cricket is a "Made in India" game that provides an authentic & surreal cricket experience. The team behind Real Cricket is Nautilus Mobile, an Indian developer based in Pune.

Football

Football is a game involving two teams of 11 players who try to maneuver the ball into the other team's goal without using their hands or arms
